## 实现要点

- `global-shortcut` 插件：`register("CmdOrCtrl+Shift+N", callback)`
- `TrayIconBuilder`：创建托盘图标
- `on_tray_icon_event`：点击恢复窗口
- `onCloseRequested`：拦截关闭，改为隐藏
